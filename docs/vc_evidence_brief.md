# VC Evidence Brief

The strongest investor story is not "quantum magic." It is measurable optimization value per dollar.

## Charts That Matter

- Cost per valid solution against cloud quantum, commercial optimization, and open-source solver baselines.
- Time to first feasible and time to best feasible on TSP, Max-Cut, MKP, and portfolio-like workloads.
- Quality gap versus best known solution under fixed budgets: 1 second, 10 seconds, 60 seconds, 10 minutes.
- Developer adoption evidence: install-to-first-run time, free-tier conversion triggers, SDK examples run rate.
- Infrastructure advantage: laptop/API access versus specialized hardware or paid QPU queue.

## Claims That Could Matter Commercially

- "Best cost-normalized time-to-good-enough on routing-like workloads."
- "API-first solver that lets developers add optimization without solver infrastructure."
- "Quantum-inspired optimization surface with reproducible public evidence and no hardware lock-in."

The market will not reward vague superiority. It will reward narrow, repeatable wins in expensive workflows.
